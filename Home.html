Run, Save, and Earn: Revolutionizing Fitness and Finance

Our Mission

  • Build tools that encourage a healthy lifestyle and financial discipline by merging physical activity with innovative savings mechanisms

Our Vision

  • We seek to create a community where fitness and financial well-being go hand-in-hand, promoting a culture of commitment and reward through active living and smart saving

What we do

  • Social and Financial Motivation Appliation: We integrate running clubs with web3 wallets, allowing members to stake USDC for yields, track their runs via Strava, and earn rewards for meeting activity goals while fostering accountability through no-loss games.

  • IRL Running Clubs: We host running clubs on Strava and have in-person run meetups for our members. We use these sessions to get feedback on the Run Money user experience as well as an ​opportunity to onboard new Run Money Club Members.
